Strength vs Hypertrophy Weightlifting Tips

Strength Training

  • Low reps, higher weight.
  • Focus on driving hard at the bottom of the lift (right before the concentric movement).
  • More compound movements.

Hypertrophy Training

  • Higher reps, lower weight.
  • Focus on the eccentric movement (lowering the weight).
  • More isolation movements.

Always Remember

  • Warm up before you start lifting weights.
    • I like to do 1-2 warmup sets before getting into my working sets.
  • Progressively Overload.
    • Always strive to increase the weight little by little as time goes on. Not only important for strength training but hypertrophy as well.
  • Rest between sets.
    • Resting between sets is important to allow your muscles to recover and perform optimally.
  • Stay hydrated.
    • Drink water before, during, and after your workout.
